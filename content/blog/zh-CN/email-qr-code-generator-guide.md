An **email qr code generator** opens a prefilled email draft after scan, which reduces friction for support requests, partnership inquiries, and sales leads.

## High-Intent Email QR Scenarios

- Support and help desks
- B2B sales brochures
- Event inquiry booths
- Printed onboarding materials

## How to Make an Email QR Code

1. Prepare a `mailto:` link with recipient and subject
2. Open [ToolPort QR Code Generator](/tools/qr-code?template=email)
3. Paste and generate
4. Download and place on your material
5. Test on iOS Mail, Gmail, and Android clients

## Email QR Format Tips

- Keep subject concise and action-oriented
- URL-encode spaces and symbols in subject/body
- Include fallback text email address below the code

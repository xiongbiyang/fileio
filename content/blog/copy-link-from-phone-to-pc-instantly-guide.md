Need to **copy link from phone to pc instantly**? A shared browser clipboard room is faster than sending yourself messages or opening extra apps.

## Why This Is Useful

- Share URLs across devices in seconds
- Avoid chat app clutter for small tasks
- No account switching between devices
- Works in mobile and desktop browsers

## Fast Copy-Link Workflow

1. Open [ToolPort Online Clipboard](/tools/clipboard) on both devices
2. Create a room on one device
3. Join the same room on the other device
4. Paste link on phone and copy on PC

## Practical Tips

- Use a short room ID for quick join
- Clear room after finishing
- Keep one tab pinned for frequent use

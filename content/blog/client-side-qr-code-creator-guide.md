If you care about privacy, a **frictionless client-side QR code creator** is the safest default.

With ToolPort, QR generation happens in your browser, so your content is not uploaded for server-side rendering.

## What "Client-side" Means

- The QR image is generated by browser code on your device
- Your input stays local during generation
- You can export directly as PNG, SVG, or WebP

Use it here: [ToolPort QR Code Tool](/tools/qr-code)

## Best Use Cases

- Private links for internal docs
- Contact cards and personal info
- Temporary campaign URLs before final launch
- Prototyping branded QR with logo and color

## Quick Checklist

1. Paste URL or text
2. Set contrast-safe colors
3. Add logo if needed
4. Export and test scan on two devices

Typing passwords is slow, especially for guests and events. A **WiFi QR code generator** lets people scan and connect in seconds, which is why terms like **wifi password qr code** and **share wifi qr code** are growing fast.

## Why Use a WiFi QR Code

- No manual password typing
- Fewer input errors
- Faster onboarding for guests
- Great for cafes, studios, and rentals

## How to Create a WiFi QR Code

1. Open [ToolPort QR Code Generator](/tools/qr-code?template=wifi)
2. Enter WiFi string format or a connection instruction link
3. Customize color and logo if needed
4. Download as PNG or SVG
5. Print or display it near your router

## Best Practices

- Use high contrast colors for better scan rate
- Keep code at least 3x3 cm when printed
- Test on iOS and Android before publishing
- Regenerate if your password changes

## PNG vs SVG for WiFi QR

- PNG: quick sharing in chat or social
- SVG: best for print, posters, and high-res assets
